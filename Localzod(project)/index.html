Who We Are

LocalZod is a product delivery software. Online product delivery is an interaction of 4 parties — customers who order products, merchants who sell the products, delivery agents who physically deliver the order from the merchants to the customer, and the software which facilitates this entire process while ensuring a smooth experience. LocalZod makes sure this process is done seamlessly.

To make the software even more powerful, Hyperzod was build from scratch using the latest technologies and a new philosophy which focuses on modularity. This enabled us to revisit every nook and corner of the software and further improve the software.
